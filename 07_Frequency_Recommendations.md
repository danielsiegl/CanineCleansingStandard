# **7. Frequency Recommendations**  

- **Short-Coated Breeds:** Every 4–6 weeks.  
- **Long-Coated Breeds:** Every 3–4 weeks.  
- **Double-Coated Breeds:** Every 6–8 weeks (or as needed).  
- **Special Conditions:** Clean as advised by a veterinarian.
