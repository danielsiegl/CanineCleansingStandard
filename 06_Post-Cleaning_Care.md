# **6. Post-Cleaning Care**  

## **6.1. Grooming**  
- Brush coat to detangle and restore natural shine.  
- Inspect ears and clean gently with a vet-approved solution.  

## **6.2. Reward**  
- Offer treats or affection to positively reinforce the experience.
- A proposed change.
